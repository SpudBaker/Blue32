32 Blue builds high quality, affordable, low maintenance business applications for the SME market.

The company was formed in 2017 by Richard Baker, BSc(Hons), Prince 2 practitioner and ITIL expert. Richard has worked in IT for 20 years in Blue Chip and SME organisations most recently as a Head of IT.

The characteristics that set 32 Blue apart are:
  • Customer engagement is key
  • Solutions must not be just functionally rich but must also be responsive, available, and trouble free allowing for satisfied and effective users
  • Use of enterpise standard open-source technology
  • Quality is vital
  • A transparent cost and delivery model with appropriate project management
